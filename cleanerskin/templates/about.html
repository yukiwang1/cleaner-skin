About Cleaner Skin


Cleaner Skin is a quick and easy tool that analyzes the contents of skincare products for ingredients that may have potentially harmful effects. This tool was created to help consumers become more aware about the ingredients in their everyday skincare products.


How Ingredients are Rated

Here are the thresholds used to determine the risk level of ingredients:

Low

May cause skin irritation in certain individuals.

Moderate

Risk of contaminants with toxic chemicals are carcinogens or have other adverse effects.

High

Contains ingredients that are known carcinogens or known to release carcinogens.



Disclaimer: Results of this tool are meant to raise awareness and does not represent an exhaustive list of all potential health risks. For specific questions about health risks, please consult your dermatologist.
